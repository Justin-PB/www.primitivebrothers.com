Quick Guide

Short on time? These are the most important tips:

  • Use all Coupon Codes to get around 17,000 free gems. Spend these gems wisely on the next steps.
  • Level all muscles to 25 first. This boosts your speed and is the top priority.
  • Think about buying the Beginner's Pack and the No Ads option. These provide big benefits.
  • If you are new, avoid buying chests (except for totems) until you understand the game better.
  • Join a guild right away. Use public chat to find a helpful guild. You can earn 300 guild tokens daily.
  • Do Alchemy every day to improve your totems. Try to turn 8 lesser totems into Rabbit Totems daily.
  • Always complete daily tasks like Guild Buffs, PVP, Boss Island, Alchemy, Club, and Hospital activities.
  • Upgrade your totems to level 530 as quickly as you can.
  • Spend market tokens only on Totem Chests for better progress. Totem Chests are very important.
  • Before leveling any muscle above 25, read the full guide to understand how builds work.
  • Come back and read the full guide when you have more time for details.

Time until reset:
When the game resets, it’s time to do guild buffs and daily tasks.

Getting Started

Starting Tips

  • Spend Gems Wisely: Use gems to make your character stronger.
  • Unlock New Content: Play the game to open new things and activities.
  • Maximize Daily Activities: Do hourly and daily activities to get gems, tokens, and resources. They add up over time.

Gameplay

  • Use idle mode to collect resources.
  • Run the game all day and night for best progress.
  • Use Sleep Mode to save battery while staying online.
  • When offline, you get 1 kill per minute, up to 10 hours.

F2P and P2W

The game is free to play, but some purchases help a lot:

  • Rookie Package: Gives a permanent auto-clicker and a 2x stat boost for Bro, Turret, and HP regeneration. Very important.
  • No Ads Forever: Removes ads, gives daily gems and chests instantly, and makes "Thor's Might" easy to refresh. Highly recommended.
  • Extra Totem Deck: Makes it easy to switch between farm and push skills.
  • Daily 300 Gems: Gives you gems every day.

Dictionary

Here are some words and phrases you might hear:

  • Acc: Short for accessories.
  • Alc: Short for alchemy.
  • Comp: Short for companions.
  • Def: Short for defense.
  • Eva: Short for evasion.
  • Farm Stage: A stage with better drop rates. There are two types: gear farms and accessory farms.
  • KPO (Kills Per Overclock): Used for 'turret builds' to measure farming speed. To find your KPO, turn on an overclock and count the kills until it ends.
  • KPR (Kills Per Rage): Used for 'bro builds' to measure farming speed. To find your KPR, turn on a rage and count the kills until it ends.
  • Glyphs (Formally known as Magic Glyphs / MG ): You can get these at stage 3001. You can infuse magic powder into them to recieve a boost in power.
  • Pnt: This is the defense penetration skill. It lets you do damage through a monster's defense.
  • RM / Runes (Rune Monument): The first type of glyphs you get. You can upgrade them using runes. You should complete your RSD everyday.
  • RSD (Rune Shard Dungeon): This dungeon gives rune shards for making runes.
  • The Space Wall: A point in middle game where progress first slows down. This is before reaching space (T76) and ends at stage 10641.
  • The Multiverse Wall: A point late in the game where progress slows down for the 2nd time. This is before reaching the Multiverse (T150) and ends at stage 26390.

Basic Mechanics

Civilization and Caps

  • When you reach a new civilization, you get stronger and find more Stone and Rice.
  • Use stones to level up Bro, Turret, Hut, and Juliette to advance civilizations.
  • Civilization levels limit some parts of the game, so advancing is important.

Farming and Pushing

  • Farm stages have the best drop rates before resetting. This makes drops better by one stage.
  • Farm equipment at farm stages to get stronger and move to the next stage.
  • There are different farm stages for Gear, Accessories, Turret, and Hut parts. Accessories are the most important.
  • Skip farm stages with active play until stage 2000. After stage 2000, farm and push from farm to farm.
  • Try to have at least 1.5 times your current farm stage's power to make pushing easier. Less power means slower farming or not reaching the next stage.

Totem Skills

  • Totem skills make different parts of the game better, like damage and drop rates.
  • You get 1 skill point for every 2 successful totem upgrades.
  • Use different skills for farming and pushing.
  • If you have an extra totem deck, create 2 skill setups for easy swapping.

Alchemy

  • Alchemy lets you change equipment or resources into better items.
  • Use 100 points for free and refresh 3 times daily for 100, 200, and 300 gems.

Wigs and Masks

  • Wigs and masks give random stat boosts of 1-5%.
  • Use dice to change stats and boosts randomly.
  • For farming, focus on Strength (STR). For pushing, use Evasion (EVA) for slow bosses and Defense (DEF) for fast bosses.

Ranking

  • Ranking is based on the highest stage you reach.
  • Server-rank: The first number in your name.
  • Global-rank: The second number in your name.
  • Global rank changes your name color.

The Wall of Space

  • Space starts at stage 10641.
  • You need to reach space to go past civilization 75.
  • Bosses get much stronger between stages 10000 and 10650.
  • You need a balanced build with good power and HP.

The Wall of The Multiverse

  • The Multiverse starts at stage 26390.
  • You need to reach the Multiverse to go past civilization 150.
  • Bosses get much stronger between stages 25001 and 26390.
  • You need a specialized build with good power and HP.

Battle Mechanics

Boss Battles

  • Defeat the boss in 12 seconds. If you don't, you lose.
  • Juliette adds an energy bar for boss battles. Keep Juliette's level at least 30 levels higher than Bro's to keep Bro attacking.
  • Use totem skill points to make bosses weaker and increase your damage.

Thor's Might

  • Deals 5-10% of the boss's HP per try. Use it 3 times per battle to reduce 15-30% of the boss's HP.
  • With Permanent No-Ads, it's very useful in battles.

Uncle

  • Uncle also reduces a percentage of the boss's HP.
  • Fully upgraded, Uncle can remove up to 19.2% of the boss's HP. This helps with the "Space push."
  • New players should upgrade Uncle with gems when the success rate is high. Later, focus on other parts of the game until you have a strong build.

Stat Amplifiers

Totems

  • Totems boost different stats. You can get them by completing stages in the Bronificent Tower.
  • Every 2 upgrades give you 1 skill point.
  • Totems make you much stronger, just like muscle enhancements.

Rune Glyph

  • Rune Glyphs boost different stats.
  • Change Rune Shards in the Rune Factory. Get Rune Shards by completing stages in the Rune Shard Dungeon.

Magic Glyph

  • Unlock Magic Glyphs at stage 3001. They boost different stats.
  • Every completed tier gives a 1% Civilization boost.
  • Upgrade the highest tier for the best stats. Then, upgrade from low to high.

Spirits

  • Spirits can cause damage to the enemy.
  • They increase all of your stats overall.
  • Generally speaking, your highest-tier spirits are usually the best regardless of secondary skill.
  • Each spirit also amplifies a specific secondary skill depending on the type you choose:
    • Rat: Increases Strength.
    • Cow: Increases HP.
    • Tiger: Increases Turret Engineering.
    • Rabbit: Increases Hut Construction.
    • Dragon: Increases Bow & Slingshot Companion Stats.
    • Snake: Increases Spear and Axe Companion Stats.
    • Horse: Increases Illustrated Handbook Stats.
    • Sheep: Increases Challenge Stats.
    • Monkey: Increases Gear Level Stats.
    • Chicken: Increases Accessory Level Stats.
    • Dog: Increases Turret Part Stats.
    • Pig: Increases Hut Material Stats.

Vehicles

Your bro rides a vehicle into battle! While the vehicle doesn't provide any benefits on its own, there's an opportunity to upgrade your vehicle using "Fuel", which can be purchased from the in-game store.

  • Upgrading your vehicle increases all stats, giving a great boost to your power.
  • Try to upgrade all your available vehicles evenly. When upgrades become more expensive, prioritize different upgrades until you unlock the next vehicle.
  • When you unlock a new vehicle, you'll find it cheaper and providing a much larger benefit initially.
  • Vehicles are a strategic way to amplify your stats, so balance your resources wisely.

Figures

Figures provide various benefits in battle. Here are some key points:

  • Earn figures by completing holiday events throughout the year.
  • Equip a figure by navigating to Artifacts and selecting the Figure tab.
  • Purchase a "Figure Box Exchange Ticket" to unlock a random figure.
  • Figures are a strategic way to enhance your stats; choose ones that complement your build.

Daily Activities

Portal: Bronificent Tower

  • Get Totem chests for each floor you complete.
  • Battles are boss battles.

Portal: Boss Challenge

  • Get resources or gems for defeating bosses.
  • Each boss you defeat gives a stat boost.
  • You can join this activity up to 5 times daily for free.

Portal: Rune Shard Dungeon

  • Get Rune Shards by defeating the "boss."
  • Change Rune Shards to Runes in the Rune Factory.
  • This activity refreshes daily, with 3 free tickets a day.
  • Rune Shards give a big power boost, making this activity very important.
  • Battles are boss battles.

Portal: Spirit Dungeon

  • Get Spirit eggs by defeating the "boss."
  • Change Spirit eggs to Spirits in the Spirit Egg hatchery.
  • This activity refreshes every 24 hours, with 3 free tries each time.
  • Spirits give a big power boost, making this activity very important.

Portal: Fuel Dungeon

  • Battle fuel barrel monsters to collect cans of fuel.
  • Fuel can be used to upgrade your vehicle and gain an all-stat bonus.
  • Complete this activity daily for maximum benefit.

Portal: Brock Dungeon

  • Battle a living brother stone monster to earn brocks.
  • Brocks are used to upgrade your companions.
  • Complete this daily and follow the companion build guide for optimal progression.

Portal: Magic Dust Dungeon

  • Battle dust monsters to collect magic dust.
  • Magic dust can be used to complete glyphs (unlocked at stage 3001).
  • You can also use magic dust to transcend glyphs (unlocked at stage 7001).
  • Daily completion is recommended for steady progression.

Fishing, Club, PVP, and Guild

  • Get Fish-tokens by selling fish you catch. You get 25 tries each day.
  • Get Club-tokens by joining Club activities. You get 5 chances daily.
  • Get Honor-tokens by joining the PVP arena. You get 7 chances daily.
  • Get Guild-tokens by doing g uild quests. These reset every day.
  • Use tokens at the Market to buy resources.

Note: When getting tokens, always have +5 in your "get special token" skill. Do this before the reset to get the most PVP tokens in your daily mail.

Builds

Invest gems in a specialized build to be more efficient

  • Bro-build: Needs auto-clicker, most common and balanced build.
  • Turret-build: No auto-clicker needed, great for free players, strong start.
  • Companion-build: No auto-clicker needed, strongest but hardest to manage.

Essentials

  • 186 is the magic number for enhancements, boosting stats by 12500%.
  • Alchemy is very important for changing accessories, runes, and totems.
  • All builds need at least 300 totem points for good skill coverage. Detailed skill setups are on individual build pages.

Companion-Build

As of 12/1/2024, this is the strongest and most recommended build for new players following the latest update. While it requires careful management, its power is unmatched. No auto-clicker is needed since companions always attack. Keep in mind that enhancement resources are 4 times more expensive, and it relies heavily on companions' skills for boss battles.

Order of Enhancements

  1. Enhance Muscles, Turret, and Hut Parts Evenly: Upgrade your muscles, turret, and hut parts evenly until you unlock the Bow Bro. This balanced approach ensures you have a solid foundation.
  2. Daily Totem Management: Use daily tokens from fishing, club, PvP, and guild to convert them into Rabbit Totems via the Alchemy workshop. Continue using relevant totems like Mantis and Wolf while saving and converting others into Rabbit Totems.
  3. Enhance Bow Bro to 186: Upgrade the Bow Bro companion to 186 for significant boosts to your build.
  4. Maximize Totem Enhancements: Aim to enhance your total totem upgrades to 530 to maximize effectiveness.
  5. Enhance Slingshot Bro to 186: Upgrade Slingshot Bro to 186, as this companion synergizes well with Bow Bro to increase your power.
  6. Get Spear and Axe Bro to 186: Upgrade Spear Bro and Axe Bro to 186 to strengthen your companion team further.
  7. Enhance All Companions to 200: Once you’ve maxed out Bow, Slingshot, Spear, and Axe companions to 186, upgrade all your remaining companions to level 200.
  8. Rabbit Totem to 50, Then Wolf Totem: By this point, you should have Rabbit Totem at level 50. Start working towards upgrading your Wolf Totem to level 50.
  9. Invest Gems in Spirits: Spend spare gems on leveling up Spirits. Focus on achieving level 100 for Purple Snake, Cow, and Dragon Spirits for powerful boosts.
  10. Minimize Spending on Magic Glyphs: Avoid spending gems on Magic Glyphs unless necessary. If you need to, only invest the bare minimum to keep progressing. Skip glyphs whenever possible to save gems.
  11. Level Vehicles Evenly: Upgrade your vehicles evenly, aiming for level 20 to 25 to maintain consistent progression and resource gathering.
  12. Transcend Glyph to Level 40, Then Stop: Once you’ve transcended your glyph to level 40, stop upgrading. Beyond this point, the cost becomes too high for little benefit.

Alchemy

  • Totems are very important for this build. Convert totems in this order and make sure you do so every day. Always convert 8 totems per day, which will cost 600 gems.
    • Rabbit totems up to level 50
    • Wolf totems up to level 50
    • Rabbit totems up to level 80
    • Wolf totems up to level 80
    • Crow (Health) or Chick (Stength for totem damage) totems up a level 80.

Bro-Build

Bro-Build Playstyle

  • Balanced build that improves both strength and HP.
  • Needs a permanent auto-clicker because Bro does not attack by himself.
  • High risk, high reward. Upgrading accessories needs 2 same materials but has a lower success chance.

Order of Enhancements

  • Enhance all muscles to 25 for maximum attack speed.
  • Enhance finger muscle to 186-250 for strong rings.
  • Enhance ear muscle to 186-250 for strong earrings.
  • Enhance biceps muscle to 186-200 for strong weapons.
  • Enhance finger, ear, and biceps muscles to 250.
  • Enhance forearm muscle to 250.
  • Enhance trapezius muscle to 250.

Alchemy

  • Change accessories into rings to increase upgrade chances.
  • Convert runes into Yellow runes to improve accessory stats.
  • Convert Totems to boost specific accessories like Mantis (ring), Monkey (bracelet), Snake (earring), and Rhino (necklace).

Totem Awakening (Optional)

Though expensive, adding the totem lightning attack can be useful as it gets stronger with Bro's power. You need 60 extra totem points for full skill coverage. More effective for long farm sessions.

Accessories Chests (Optional)

Higher levels increase accessory drop rates. Higher levels close to your civilization give better drops than farming. It’s expensive but gives steady accessories.

Turret-Build

A strong but slow-attacking build that doesn't need an auto-clicker since the turret always attacks. Great for free-to-play players, it depends on the Engineer's skill to defeat bosses.

Order of Enhancements

  • Enhance all turret parts to 25 to get the highest turret attack speed.
  • (Optional) Enhance all muscles to 25 for better HP.
  • Enhance turret part A to 186-200.
  • Enhance forearm muscle to 186-200 to improve bracelet (best HP).
  • Enhance turret parts B, C, and D to 186-200.
  • Enhance all turret parts and forearm muscle to 250.

Engineer

This build relies on the Engineer's skill to defeat bosses. Upgrade it to at least level 40 to improve the skill's effectiveness. Each level after 40 makes it stronger.

Alchemy

  • Change accessories into bracelets to boost upgrade chances.
  • Convert runes into Green runes to improve turret stats.
  • Convert totems to enhance specific turret parts like Fox (turret part A), Phoenix (turret part B), Lion (turret part C), and Owl (turret part D).

Turret Chests (Optional)

Higher levels increase turret part drop rates. Higher levels are better than farming for getting useful drops.
